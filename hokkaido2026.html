🍽️ Food & Cafés
Nijo Fish Market – Kaisendon
Freshest uni & ikura bowls. Only 8 seats — arrive 7am.
🦀 Seafood¥2,900–5,000⚡ 7am open
Ramen Alley (Ganso Ramen Yokocho)
Miso ramen with snow crab. Rich buttery Hokkaido broth.
🍜 Ramen¥1,000–1,500
Soup Curry Garaku
Customisable spice. Chicken + Hokkaido veg.
🍛 Curry¥1,300–1,800
Daruma Honten – Genghis Khan BBQ
Legendary mutton BBQ. 12 seats only — queue before opening, no reservations.
🥩 BBQ¥2,500–3,500
SATURDAYS Chocolate Factory & Café
Bean-to-bar chocolate. Best mocha in Sapporo.
☕ Café¥800–1,500
Parfait Coffee Sake Sato
Sapporo's shime parfait tradition. Opens 5pm.
🍨 Dessert¥1,500–2,500
Kinotoya Bake ⭐
Hokkaido's most famous fresh cheese tart — crispy outside, molten inside. Queue early, sells out fast.
🧀 Dessert¥230/tart⚡ Sells out!
Sapporo Beer Garden & Museum
Historic red-brick brewery. Free museum tour. Famous Genghis Khan BBQ with unlimited beer.
🥩 BBQ+BeerMuseum free · BBQ ¥3,500+
Yoshimi Soup Curry
Pioneer of Sapporo soup curry. Massive portions, custom spice levels. Try the chicken leg.
🍛 Curry¥1,400–1,900
Boulangerie Pignon
Top-rated Sapporo bakery — buttery croissants and Hokkaido milk bread. Great breakfast stop.
🥐 Bakery¥200–600
🛍️ Shopping & Sights
Tanukikoji Shopping Arcade
7-block covered street: souvenir shops, 100¥ stores, trinkets.
🛍️ ShoppingFree entry
Odori Park & TV Tower
1.5km park. Tower observation deck. Great morning walk.
🌿 ParkTower ¥700
Shiroi Koibito Park
Famous cookie factory as theme park. Iconic white choc cookies.
🍪 Sweets¥600 entry
Hokkaido Shrine (Hokkaido Jingu)
Sapporo's most important shrine inside Maruyama Park forest. Serene in summer. Combine with Maruyama Zoo nearby.
⛩️ ShrineFree
Moerenuma Park ⭐
Stunning sculpture park designed by Isamu Noguchi. Glass Pyramid, Mount Moere, play fountain. Half-day worthy.
🎨 Art+NatureFree
Hokkaido University Campus
Beautiful ginkgo avenue and botanical gardens. Charming European-style campus — great morning stroll.
🌿 CampusFree
Susukino Night District
Sapporo's lively entertainment district — neon-lit bars, izakayas, karaoke and late-night ramen.
🌃 NightlifeVaries

🌸 Nature & Activities
Farm Tomita ⭐
Most iconic lavender farm. FREE. Late June: early lavender + poppies/lupins in full bloom.
💜 LavenderFree
Shirogane Blue Pond (Biei)
Surreal turquoise pond. Best in morning light. Nearby: Shirahige Falls.
💙 NatureFree
Ningle Terrace
Magical woodland artisan market — quirky hand-crafted trinkets.
🎪 QuirkyFree to browse
Shikisai-no-Oka (Biei)
15-hectare flower hill with alpaca farm. NOROCCO tractor rides.
🦙 FlowersFree
Furano Cheese Factory
Cheese + butter making experience. Wine Cheddar is a great souvenir.
🧀 Experience¥500–1,200
Ken & Mary Tree (Biei) ⭐
Iconic lone elm made famous by a 1972 Nissan ad. Photogenic on the Patchwork Road drive.
🌳 ScenicFree
Seven Stars Tree (Biei)
Solitary oak on a hilltop — appeared on a Seven Stars cigarette pack. Stunning 360° views.
🌳 ScenicFree
Mild Seven Hill (Biei)
Row of larch trees against a flower-patchwork backdrop. One of Biei's most photographed spots.
🌿 ScenicFree
Furano Wine House
Winery on a hill with vineyard views. Sample Furano reds and whites. Great sunset spot.
🍷 WineryTasting ¥200–500

Biei Café Ruhe

🍈 Local¥1,500–3,000

🌋 Activities
Mt Usu Ropeway
6-min cable car up active volcano. See Showa-Shinzan lava dome.
🌋 Volcano¥1,800/person
Lake Toya Fireworks 🎆
Nightly Apr–Oct. Watch at 20:30 from lakefront in yukata.
🎆 FestivalFree
SUP / Canoe on Lake Toya
Paddleboard on caldera lake with volcano backdrop. Beginner-friendly.
🏄 Water¥5,000–8,000
Nishiyama Crater Trail
Free 1.6km trail — buildings still damaged from 2000 eruption. Do this morning before Hakodate.
🥾 HikeFree
Lakeside Sculpture Promenade
58 sculptures along the shoreline. Beautiful evening walk.
🎨 Art+NatureFree
Showa Shinzan Lava Dome
Active lava dome that rose from a wheat field in 1943. Walk the base trail after the ropeway — eerie and fascinating.
🌋 VolcanoFree (base trail)
Usu Volcanic Science Museum
Interactive museum about Lake Toya's eruption history. Photos and relics from the 2000 eruption. Very educational.
🏛️ Museum¥600
Lake Toya Boat Cruise
30-min cruise around the lake's four central islands (Nakajima islands). Spot deer and wildlife.
⛵ Cruise¥1,500/person
🍽️ Food & Onsen Town
Toyako Onsen Monzen Street
Evening market street in the onsen town — grilled scallops, corn, and Hokkaido street food. Lively after 6pm.
🌽 Street Food¥200–800/item⚡ Evenings only
Ryokan Kaiseki Dinner
If your Lake Toya hotel offers kaiseki, do it — multi-course seasonal Japanese dinner with local Hokkaido ingredients.
🍱 KaisekiOften included in stay

Morning Market Kaisendon ⭐

Live Squid Fishing (Ekini Ichiba)
Catch your own squid, prepared as sashimi before your eyes!
🦑 Experience~¥600+
Lucky Pierrot Burger
Hakodate-only local chain. Order the Chinese Chicken Burger.
🍔 Local¥500–800
Hakodate Shio Ramen
Light, clear seafood-stock broth. Try Suzuya Shokudo in market.
🍜 Ramen¥700–1,000
Snaffle's Cheese Omelette ⭐
Hakodate's most loved souvenir cake — fluffy egg omelette with rich cream cheese centre. Light and addictive.
🧀 Dessert¥250/piece · Box sets ¥1,500+
Hakodate Beer Hall
Craft brewery inside the historic Kanemori warehouse. Try the signature amber ale with fresh seafood.
🍺 Craft Beer¥700–1,200/glass
Milkissimo Ice Cream
Famous Hakodate soft serve — triple-scoop tower of Hokkaido milk, melon, and lavender. Very photogenic!
🍦 Dessert¥500–700
Hakodate Asaichi Grilled Scallops
At the morning market — vendors grill fresh scallops live on the spot. Best with butter and soy. ¥300–500 each.
🐚 Seafood¥300–500/shell⚡ 5am–noon
🏙️ Sights & Shopping
Mt Hakodate Ropeway – Night View ⭐
One of Japan's top 3 night views. Go at dusk. 3-min gondola.
🌟 Must-do¥1,500 return
Kanemori Red Brick Warehouses
Boutiques, restaurants and craft beer hall. Great souvenirs.
🏮 ShoppingFree entry
Motomachi Historic District
Foreign consulates, churches, Western buildings. Very photogenic.
🏛️ HistoryFree
Goryokaku Fort & Tower
Japan's first star-shaped Western fort. Great aerial view from tower.
⭐ FortTower ¥1,000
Haristosu Russian Orthodox Church
Stunning green-domed church built in 1916. One of Motomachi's most photographed landmarks.
⛪ HistoricFree to view exterior
Trappistine Convent & Cookies ⭐
Japan's first convent for nuns (1898). Famous butter cookies and caramels sold at the gift shop — excellent souvenir.
🍪 SweetsCookies ¥600–1,200
